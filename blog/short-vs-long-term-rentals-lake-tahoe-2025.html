Short-Term vs Long-Term Rentals in Lake Tahoe: Pros & Cons for Owners

Tahoe Peak Editorial Team Content Specialist

Lake Tahoe property owners have a choice between short-term and long-term rentals. Each option has unique benefits and challenges. Here's what to consider in 2025.

Short-Term Rentals: Pros & Cons

Short-term rentals (STRs) can offer high returns but come with more management and regulation.

  • Higher nightly rates and income potential
  • Flexibility for owner use
  • Increased wear and tear
  • Subject to local STR regulations and taxes

Long-Term Rentals: Pros & Cons

Stability and Predictability

Long-term tenants provide steady income and less turnover.

  • Consistent monthly cash flow
  • Lower management demands
  • Less flexibility for owner use
  • Potential for below-market rents in long leases

Tenant Screening & Lease Terms

Careful screening and clear lease terms are essential for long-term success.

  • Thorough background checks
  • Clear maintenance responsibilities
  • Defined lease renewal and termination policies
  • Legal compliance with California rental laws

Which Strategy is Right for You?

Consider your financial goals, risk tolerance, and desired involvement. Consult with a local property manager to determine the best fit for your Lake Tahoe property in 2025.

  • Analyze projected income and expenses
  • Review local rental regulations
  • Factor in personal use and flexibility
  • Plan for long-term property value growth
