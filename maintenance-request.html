Submit Your Maintenance Request

Important Information

Please note that our maintenance team will respond to your request based on the urgency level and type of issue. For emergencies that require immediate attention, please call our emergency line at 530.994.8882.

Emergency Situations

  • Fire or smoke
  • Major flooding or water leaks
  • Gas leaks or suspicious odors
  • No heat during winter months
  • No functioning toilet in the unit
  • Security or safety issues

What to Expect

  • Confirmation email upon submission
  • Response within 24-48 hours for non-emergencies
  • Scheduling of maintenance visit
  • Follow-up after completion
  • Option to provide feedback

Need Immediate Assistance?
