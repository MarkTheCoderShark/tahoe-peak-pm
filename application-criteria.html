Application Criteria

Understanding our rental application requirements

Income Requirements

Applicants must demonstrate a combined gross income of at least 2.5 times the monthly rent.

  • Income verification required (pay stubs, bank statements, etc.)
  • Self-employed applicants must provide additional documentation
  • Co-signers may be considered on a case-by-case basis

Credit Requirements

A minimum credit score of 700 is required for all applicants.

  • Full credit report will be obtained
  • Bankruptcy must be discharged for at least 2 years
  • No outstanding collections or judgments

Application Fee

A non-refundable application fee of $40 per adult applicant is required.

  • Covers credit and background check
  • Processing time is typically 2-3 business days
  • Payment accepted via credit card or money order

Required Documentation

  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Proof of income (last 2 months)
  • Previous landlord references
  • Proof of employment or job offer letter

Rental History

  • Minimum 2 years of positive rental history
  • No prior evictions
  • No outstanding balances to previous landlords

Background Check

  • Criminal background check required
  • Employment verification
  • Previous address verification
