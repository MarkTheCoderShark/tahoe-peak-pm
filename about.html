About Lake Tahoe Property Management

Your trusted partner for property management services in South Lake Tahoe, Incline Village, and surrounding areas since 2010

Our Lake Tahoe Story

Dedicated to excellence in property management and real estate services

Founded in 2010, Tahoe Peak Property Management began as a small family business with a vision to provide exceptional property management services in Lake Tahoe. What started with just a few properties has grown into a comprehensive service managing hundreds of properties across the region.

Our journey has been driven by our commitment to excellence, integrity, and the unique needs of Lake Tahoe property owners. We've built our reputation on trust, reliability, and personalized service.
