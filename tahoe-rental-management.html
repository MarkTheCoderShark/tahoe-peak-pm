Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you include in your management fee?

+

Our management fee includes comprehensive services such as marketing, guest screening, 24/7 guest support, maintenance coordination, cleaning supervision, financial reporting, and more. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

How do you handle property maintenance and emergencies?

+

We provide 24/7 emergency response and have established relationships with trusted local vendors. We handle routine maintenance, emergency repairs, and implement preventive maintenance programs tailored to Lake Tahoe's unique climate.

How do you maximize my property's rental income?

+

We use dynamic pricing algorithms that adjust rates based on seasonal demand, local events, and market conditions. Our professional photography, optimized listings, and marketing strategies ensure maximum visibility on rental platforms.

Can I still use my property myself?

+

Absolutely. We offer flexible owner usage policies and an easy-to-use owner portal where you can block dates for personal use. We'll ensure your property is prepared for your arrival and cleaned after your departure.

What communication can I expect as an owner?

+

We provide monthly financial statements, occupancy reports, and maintenance updates. Your dedicated property manager is available via phone or email for any questions, and our owner portal gives you 24/7 access to your property's performance data.

How do you handle the unique challenges of Lake Tahoe properties?

+

Our team specializes in Lake Tahoe property management and understands the unique challenges like snow removal, winterization, local regulations, and seasonal market fluctuations. We implement proactive measures to protect your investment year-round.
