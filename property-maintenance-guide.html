Why Lake Tahoe Properties Need Special Care

Lake Tahoe's unique climate and environmental conditions require specialized maintenance approaches. From heavy snowfall in winter to intense sun exposure in summer, our properties face unique challenges that need expert attention.

Seasonal Maintenance Checklist

Winter Maintenance

  • Snow removal and ice management
  • Heating system inspection
  • Roof snow load monitoring
  • Pipe insulation checks
  • Emergency preparedness

Spring Maintenance

  • Snow damage assessment
  • Gutter cleaning and maintenance
  • Landscape cleanup
  • HVAC system check
  • Exterior inspection

Proper gutter maintenance is crucial for preventing water damage. Consider installing professional gutter protection systems to avoid the hassle of frequent gutter cleaning and protect your property from water damage year-round.

Summer Maintenance

  • Air conditioning service
  • Fire prevention measures
  • Landscape maintenance
  • Deck and patio care
  • Pest control

Fall Maintenance

  • Leaf removal
  • Winter preparation
  • Gutter cleaning
  • Weather stripping
  • Fireplace inspection
